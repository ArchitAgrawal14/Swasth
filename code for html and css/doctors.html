Top Cardiologist in india

1. Dr. Devi Prasad Shetty, Bangalore

Founder, Chairman, senior consultant cardiac surgeon

Narayana Institute of Cardiac Sciences, Bangalore.

Qualification: MBBS, MS, FRCS (Royal College of Surgeons of England)

Experience: 35+ Years

Awards:

  • Padma Bhushan 2012
  • Padma Shri, 2004
  • Dr. B.C ROY Award 2003

Dr. Devi Shetty is a great and experienced cardiac surgeon. He is one of the most accomplished doctors globally in bypass grafting operation on a beating heart. He has performed over 15,000 heart surgeries.

He founded Narayana health in 2001, where surgeons perform 30 major surgeries daily at a low cost.

He performed the first dynamic cardiomyoplasty in Asia.

For the first time in India, he used a left ventricular assist device to support the failing heart temporarily.

He became the first to perform a neonatal (newly born baby) open heart surgery in India. He performed open-heart surgery on a nine-day-old baby in 1990.

He has expertise in pediatric cardiac surgery, Pulmonary Endarterectomy, Ross procedure, Redo Heart Surgery, Surgery for Aortic Aneurysms.

Reference: Narayana Health

2. Dr. Ashok Seth, New Delhi

Interventional cardiologist,

Fortis Escorts Heart Institute, New Delhi

Qualification: MBBS, MD, FRCP, MRCP, Fellowship USA.

Experience: 38 years

Awards:

  • Padma Bhushan
  • Padma Shri
  • Lifetime Achievement Award for clinical and preventive cardiology.

Ashok Seth is the most respected cardiologist in India. The renowned cardiologist for performing over 50,000 angiograms and 20,000 angioplasties.

Limca's book of records featured him for performing one of the highest angiographies and angioplasties in the world.

He has trained 500 cardiologists for advanced techniques of angioplasty in India and around the world.

He is the first cardiologist in the Asia-Pacific region to perform an angioscopic and directional atherectomy.

He has pioneered many angioplasty techniques like bioabsorbable stents, thrombectomy devices, transcatheter aortic valve implantation.

Reference: Fortis Escorts

Top Oncologist in India

1. Dr. Sameer Bakhshi: Renowned Pediatric Oncologist at AIIMS, New Delhi

Leading Advances in Pediatric Cancer Care

Meet Dr. Sameer Bakhshi, an esteemed figure in the field of Pediatric Oncology at AIIMS, New Delhi. With a robust educational background, over 21 years of dedicated service, and a current position as Professor and Head, he specializes in pediatric oncology (childhood cancer), eye cancer, blood cancer, and bone cancer. Dr. Bakhshi's impactful leadership is evident through his lead on the Pediatric Oncology Project since 2008, ensuring comprehensive care with social worker support. Collaborating with Can Kids, he strengthens family support systems, showcasing a holistic approach. Dr. Bakhshi's dedication extends to active contributions to pediatric oncology advocacy and research, solidifying his respected position in the medical community and becoming one of the top 10 oncologists at AIIMS, New Delhi.

Reference: Dr. Sameer Bakhshi - AIIMS Profile

2. Dr. Ramesh S Bilimagga: Transformative Figure in Radiation Oncology

Pioneering Excellence for Over 4 Decades

Meet Dr. Ramesh S Bilimagga, a distinguished radiation oncologist with an impressive 46 years of experience. Currently serving as a senior consultant at HCG Cancer Center in KR Bangalore, he specializes in Radiation Oncology for head and neck, breast, and cervix. Dr. Bilimagga is not only a certified CyberKnife specialist but has also introduced groundbreaking hyperthermia treatment. With a prolific academic record of 43 publications, he continues to actively contribute to the field. Holding prestigious positions such as National President of AROI (Association of Radiation Oncologists of India) and President of IAHOM (Indian Association of Hyperthermic Oncology and Medicine), Dr. Bilimagga, recognized with the Dr. B.C Roy award, stands as a transformative figure shaping the landscape of radiation oncology in India.

Reference: Dr. Ramesh D Bilimagga - HCG Profile

3. Dr. Rajendra Toprani: Pioneer in Head and Neck Surgical Oncology

Transforming Patient Care with Expertise

Dr. Rajendra Toprani, Head of Head and Neck Surgical Oncology at HCG Cancer Centre, Ahmedabad, stands as a leading expert in the field. With a comprehensive education—MBBS, MS (Gen Surgery), and MCh (Surgical Oncology)—he specializes in treating oral and laryngeal cancers, thyroid cancer, and parotid tumors. Boasting extensive experience, Dr. Toprani is acclaimed for his proficiency in voice-preserving laryngectomies, Transoral Endoscopic Laser Surgery (TOLS), and minimally invasive robotic surgeries. His journey includes advanced training from MSKCC (Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, Chennai), New York, USA, and specialized programs at Philadelphia, highlighting his commitment to staying at the forefront of surgical oncology. Dr. Toprani's transformative contributions redefine patient care in Head and Neck Oncology, being one of the top 10 oncologists in India.

Reference: Dr. Rajendra Toprani - HCG Profile
